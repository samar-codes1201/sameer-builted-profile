Sameer Builtaid (P) Ltd. is a premier government-licensed contractor delivering turnkey solutions in Fire Protection, Electrification, and Civil Engineering.

Building with Precision & Integrity

Formerly known as "Sameer Brothers," our journey began in 1996 serving mass housing projects in Dwarka and Noida. In 2004, we evolved into Sameer Builtaid (P) Ltd., expanding our technical prowess.

Led by Directors Mr. Rizwan Khan & Mr. Irfan Khan , we operate with a fully equipped infrastructure including hydraulic testing machines and precision tools to ensure every project meets rigorous safety standards.

  • "A" Class Electrical License (No. 3541)

  • Civil & Sanitation Works

Our Mission

To execute turnkey engineering projects with uncompromising safety standards and technical precision. We are dedicated to delivering robust Fire Fighting, Electrical, and Civil infrastructure that ensures the safety and longevity of every building we touch.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted infrastructure partner in North India, recognized for integrity, timely delivery, and the seamless integration of modern safety technologies in residential and commercial developments.
